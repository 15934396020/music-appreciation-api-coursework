Music listeners need structured tools to capture their appreciation

Music listeners interpret tracks personally — but simple catalogues fail to capture that richness

Music enthusiasts do more than just listen: they rate, tag, categorise, and curate tracks into meaningful collections. This project addresses the gap by providing a backend service where every interaction is captured as structured, queryable data.

Problem

No lightweight API exists for structured music appreciation data. Existing platforms focus heavily on streaming and opaque recommendation algorithms.

Opportunity

Reviews, tags, and collections represent rich user-generated content that can be analysed and queried to provide deeper insights into music catalogues.

Solution & Approach

A RESTful API that makes appreciation data first-class citizens. The approach focuses entirely on metadata and user interaction, intentionally excluding audio streaming to maintain a clean, data-driven architecture.
